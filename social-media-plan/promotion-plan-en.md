# Jewelry Social Media Promotion Plan (English Market)
## Target: Overseas customers, mainly English-speaking

---

## Platform Priority

| Platform | Priority | Why |
|----------|----------|-----|
| Instagram | ★★★★★ | Visual product, biggest reach |
| Pinterest | ★★★★☆ | Jewelry traffic, long shelf life |
| TikTok | ★★★★☆ | Young audience, viral potential |
| X/Twitter | ★★★☆☆ | Quick updates, community |

---

## Content Mix (Phase 1: Build Account)

- 40% Product showcase (clean photos, minimal background)
- 25% Lifestyle/outfit matching (daily, date, work, party)
- 20% Styling tips & jewelry knowledge
- 10% Behind the brand (packaging, sourcing)
- 5% Engagement (polls, Q&A, giveaways)

---

## Phase Plan

**Phase 1 (Month 1-2): Pure content, ZERO selling**
- Build followers, engage with similar accounts
- No links, no prices, no "buy now"

**Phase 2 (Month 3-4): Soft mentions**
- "This piece is available in my shop"
- Link in bio only

**Phase 3 (Month 5+): Traffic to MercadoLibre**
- Link in posts/comments gradually
- Monitor conversion

---

## What You Need to Prepare

1. Product photos (clean white/neutral background, natural light)
2. Instagram/Pinterest/TikTok accounts (use same name across platforms)
3. Bio with shop link (add after Phase 1)
4. Caption in English (use templates above)

---

## Photo Requirements for Product Shots

- White or light neutral background
- Natural window light (no harsh flash)
- Close-up detail shots + full piece shot
- Model shot OR flat lay (optional)
- Minimum 1080x1080px (square) or 1080x1350px (portrait)

---

## How This Works with MercadoLibre

- Phase 1-2: Build audience, no selling
- Phase 3: Start mentioning "available on MercadoLibre" in comments
- Profile bio: Add MercadoLibre shop link
- Pinterest: Each pin links to product listing
